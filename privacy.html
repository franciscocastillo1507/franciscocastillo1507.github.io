Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Introduction

AsyncLearn ("we", "our", or "us") operates the AsyncLearn mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Profile Information: Username and display preferences.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Challenges completed, scores, XP earned, streaks, accuracy, and other in-app activity to provide the gamification experience.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for performance and compatibility purposes.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage patterns collected through Firebase Analytics to help us improve the app.

Information Collected by Third Parties

  • Google AdMob: For free-tier users, AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and usage data to display relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage statistics. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide the core app experience (challenges, streaks, XP, badges, news feed).
  • To track your progress and display statistics.
  • To send push notifications (daily reminders) if you opt in.
  • To display advertisements to free-tier users.
  • To improve the app through anonymous analytics.

Data Storage and Security

  • Your account data is stored on our secure servers.
  • Authentication tokens (JWT) are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage.
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
  • Passwords are hashed and salted — we never store plain-text passwords.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Google AdMob — Ad serving for free-tier users.
  • Firebase — Anonymous analytics.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through your profile screen.
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us.
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

AsyncLearn is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Ad Tracking and Consent

For users in the EU/EEA, we display a consent form (UMP) before collecting advertising data, in compliance with GDPR. You can choose to accept or decline personalized advertising.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app or on this page with an updated date.
