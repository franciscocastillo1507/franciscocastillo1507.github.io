Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your AsyncLearn account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to support@asynclearn.com from the email address associated with your AsyncLearn account.
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your username or registered email in the body of the message.
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email once complete.
Request Account Deletion

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, username)
  • Challenge history and submissions
  • XP, streaks, badges, and level progress
  • Daily goal and energy data
  • Squad membership
  • Notification preferences

What Data May Be Retained

  • Anonymous analytics data — Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics collected by Firebase Analytics may be retained as they cannot be linked back to your account.

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose all progress, badges, and streaks. If you change your mind, you will need to create a new account.
